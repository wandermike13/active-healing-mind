### Full App Description
```
Active Healing Mind uses the power of binaural beats, guided meditation,
and the mind-body connection to help you manage pain and accelerate healing.

HOW IT WORKS
Each session guides you through a 6-step meditation protocol combining:
• Binaural beat audio (delta, theta, and alpha frequencies)
• Injury visualization — compare your injury with healthy tissue images
• Breath-guided relaxation and focused healing intention
• Before/after pain tracking to measure your progress

BINAURAL BEATS FOR HEALING
Choose from 5 research-backed frequency tracks:
• Delta 1Hz — Deep sleep and full body relaxation
• Delta 2Hz — Injury recovery and cellular repair
• Theta 4Hz — Stress and anxiety relief
• Theta 6Hz — General relaxation and calm
• Alpha 10Hz — Focus and light meditative states

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
Log your pain levels before and after every session. Watch your pain trend
chart improve over time. Celebrate milestones at 7, 30, and 100 sessions.

DESIGNED FOR
• Chronic pain sufferers
• Sports injury recovery
• Post-surgical healing support
• General stress and tension relief
• Anyone curious about the mind-body healing connection

Note: Active Healing Mind is a wellness and meditation app. It is not a
substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
Always consult your healthcare provider for medical conditions.
```
